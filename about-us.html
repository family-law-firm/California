About Us

We pride ourselves on our long-standing reputation for professionalism, high ethical standards and our unfaltering commitment to client service.

Ideal Legal Group, Inc. is able to assist clients with a wide variety of legal needs including: Family Law, Divorce, Intellectual Property Law and other Law Services.

Keeping our clients’ best interests and budgetary concerns in mind is an ever-present goal. For this reason, we are strong proponents of alternative dispute resolution as an optional course of action for minimizing costs and resolving disputes as quickly as possible.

We are committed to meeting the evolving needs of our clients which includes enhanced services through the use of technology. Our offices are fully linked by local-area computer networks and our attorneys and staff members are proficient users of a variety of computer programs, research technology and other electronic resources.

At Ideal Legal Group, Inc., we are profoundly grateful for the opportunities we have in our community. Giving back to the communities in which we live and work is part of our firm culture. To that end, we strive to ensure that in addition to having outstanding academic credentials, each person who joins our team of professionals demonstrates respect for the contributions of others.

Every attorney at Ideal Legal Group, Inc. and many of the staff participate actively in community service, pro bono representation, and professional organizations. Current and former members of the firm serve or have served as city council member, state senator, superior court judge, municipal court judge, chamber of commerce president, King County freeholder, and state commission member and as board members of many community, non-profit organizations. We also contribute considerable hours every month to a free neighborhood legal clinic and other pro bono services. Ideal Legal Group, Inc. is proud of the communities we serve, and proud that we can give back to those communities that have given so much to us.
