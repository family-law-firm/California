Areas of Practice

Family Law

Guardianship occurs when a court orders someone other than the child’s parent to take custody of the child. It is a difficult decision to have a child raised by anyone other than the parent, but there are certain situations where the court needs to step in and decide what is best for the child.

The dissolution of marriage — regardless of financial, social or cultural background — affects children the most. Some of the most important strides in decision making during a divorce proceeding is the preparation and drafting of a custody agreement for the minor children involved.

Intellectual Property Law

A trademark is a word, phrase, symbol or design, or a combination thereof, that identifies and distinguishes the source of the goods or services of one party from those of others.

A copyright protects works of authorship, such as writings, music, and works of art that have been tangibly expressed.

Naming a product is very important to branding. The process may include identifying the objectives of branding, developing a product name, evaluating names through market testing and focus groups, choosing a final product name, and identifying it as a trademark.

Divorce

Our divorce lawyer team have vast experience in dealing with the inconsistent legal systems of different nations, especially in instances where there is no case law in a particular country or where an issue arises as to which county’s law controls the case.

Divorce can involve difficult and complex matters for families, especially when children are involved, when couples have high net worth, or when couples have citizenship in different nations. Various factors can play a crucial part in determining the complexity, duration and cost of filing for a divorce in California.

California has very specific and complex rules about how the defendant receives the papers in a legal separation proceeding.

Immigration

We help you navigate the bureaucracy of the immigration system. Every year, hundreds of thousands of people immigrate to the United States with the hope of a better life for themselves and their families, but are confronted by obstacles on their path to citizenship.

Our attorneys are experienced in handling an array of immigration procedures; including green cards and citizenship applications, work visas, H1-B Visas, deportation proceedings, asylum/refuge and Violence Against Women Act (VAWA) cases.

Restraining Orders

One of the most challenging scenarios for a family is the need for a restraining order, or protective order, which protects an individual from being physically or sexually abused, harassed, threatened or stalked by another person.

Our international family law attorneys are well versed in preparing and enforcing agreements for the different types of orders.

About Us

We pride ourselves on our long-standing reputation for professionalism, high ethical standards and our unfaltering commitment to client service. Keeping our clients’ best interests and budgetary concerns in mind is an ever-present goal. For this reason, we are strong proponents of alternative dispute resolution as an optional course of action for minimizing costs and resolving disputes as quickly as possible. We are committed to meeting the evolving needs of our clients which includes enhanced services through the use of technology. Our offices are fully linked by local-area computer networks and our attorneys and staff members are proficient users of a variety of computer programs, research technology and other electronic resources.

At Ideal Legal Group, Inc., we are profoundly grateful for the opportunities we have in our community. Giving back to the communities in which we live and work is part of our firm culture. To that end, we strive to ensure that in addition to having outstanding academic credentials, each person who joins our team of professionals demonstrates respect for the contributions of others.
